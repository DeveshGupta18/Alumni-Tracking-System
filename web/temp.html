Alumni Tracking System
Home Alumni College Directorate
This is Web based application which will keep the track of the Alumni. Alumni are those students who are passing out. Here Alumni can register/login/update their details and can participate in the events. Colleges will verify and authenticate the registered Alumni. Directorate can monitor all the colleges and alumni's registered based on criteria such as colleges, year, etc.
